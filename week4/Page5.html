This paper explores the evolution of the Green Lantern character, focusing on the transition from Alan Scott's mystical origins to Hal Jordan's high-tech, cosmic role. It examines how these changes reflect broader cultural shifts in American society, from the Golden Age's optimism to the Silver Age's complexity and social consciousness.
